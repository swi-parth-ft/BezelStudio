Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

This policy explains what Bezel Studio does with your data and how your information is handled.

Last updated: March 8, 2025

Summary

Bezel Studio is designed to keep your work on your device. Your mockups, images, and projects stay local unless you choose to export or share them. We do not sell your data.

Information You Create

Content you create in Bezel Studio (projects, canvases, images, text, and designs) is stored on your device. If you export or share your work, it leaves the app only through the action you choose (for example, saving to Photos or sharing).

Device Permissions

Bezel Studio may request access to Photos so you can import screenshots and save exports. You can control these permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment information. Purchase status is managed through Apple’s App Store systems.

Apple Services

Certain features may rely on Apple-provided frameworks (for example, Translation or Image Playground). These features operate under Apple’s platform privacy policies and device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app. The latest version will always be available here.

Contact

If you have questions, reach out at support@parthant.com.
