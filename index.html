Device Frames

Frames that feel real.

Choose from iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac frames, then drop in screenshots or videos. Add multiple frames per canvas, resize, rotate, and align with precision.

  • Multiple frames per canvas
  • Screenshot or video support
  • Snap-to alignment guides

Canvas Styling

Build atmosphere, not just backgrounds.

Start with themes, custom gradients, or photos, then layer in patterns and lighting to give every mockup depth and polish.

  • Pattern overlays with color + opacity
  • Lighting effects with adjustable intensity
  • Saved gradient presets

Typography

Type that looks composed.

Add multiple text layers and refine fonts, weights, italics, alignment, gradients, stroke, and shadows. Each line stays editable.

  • Gradient fills and strokes
  • Fine-grained shadow controls
  • Layer-aware placement

Images & Stickers

From image to sticker in one flow.

Add photos or badges, remove backgrounds, and create sticker borders with adjustable thickness and color. Save your favorites as presets.

  • Background removal
  • Custom sticker borders
  • Image Playground prompts when supported

Draw & Doodle

Sketch directly on the canvas.

Use PencilKit tools to draw, then keep your sketches as layers you can move, scale, and reorder anytime.

  • Dedicated drawing mode
  • Layer-based editing
  • Quick commit or cancel

Translation

Translate in a tap.

Translate text for the current canvas or every canvas at once, right on device. Supported languages include English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Chinese, Russian, Hindi, and Arabic.

  • Single or multi-canvas translation
  • On-device workflow
  • Fast preview refresh

Layers & Precision

Every layer, exactly where you want it.

Reorder layers, delete quickly, snap to guides, and zoom in for precise placement. Keep your layout balanced with alignment helpers.

  • Layer list with drag ordering
  • Snap-to guides
  • Zoom + pan canvas

And many more features, thoughtfully woven into every canvas.

Projects & Presets

Keep everything organized.

Projects can hold multiple canvases and styles. Duplicate, rename, search, and sort with grid or list views. Save size, gradient, and image presets.

  • Multi-canvas projects
  • Search + smart sorting
  • Preset libraries

Export & Share

Export ready. Share instantly.

Export a single canvas or all canvases together, save to Photos, or share directly from the app. Video export is supported for canvases with a video frame.

  • Image or video export
  • Save to Photos or share
  • High-quality rendering

Developer Notes

I built Bezel Studio because, as a developer, I always wanted a small studio in my pocket for turning screenshots into clean, shareable mockups. I wanted it to feel fast, tactile, and thoughtful—something I could open, design with, and ship from anywhere.

Bezel Studio is that pocket studio: frames, typography, patterns, lighting, stickers, and export all in one flow, so ideas don’t slow down when it’s time to present them.
